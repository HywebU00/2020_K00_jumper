Global Vision and Locatedness: World Literature in Chinese

  • 作者：

    Yun-Tang Hsu

  • 資料來源：

    「Journal of Traditional Chinese Internal Medicine. Vol. 18 Issue

  • 出版年度：

    2020

  • 主題術語：

    生物醫學
    肺炎
    中醫藥

  • 說明：

    AGRICOLA 農業學索摘資料庫是目前全球針對農業學相關領域收錄最齊全之索摘型電子資料庫，透過美國國家農業圖書館所編輯之專業資訊，是農業領域研究者必備之專業資料庫。主題涵蓋農業學、植物學、化學、自然資源、動物學和生態學等，收錄1970年迄今，超過850種期刊、圖書、會議論文、研究報告、政府文件和視聽媒體、電腦軟體等資料，以英語資料為主，但歐、亞和非洲系語言資料亦佔1/3以上。此資料庫亦可視為美國國家農業圖書館 (U.S. National Agricultural Library, NAL) 之館藏資料庫；每月更新。
    主題範疇：農業學、動物科學、園藝和水文學。

  • 說明 (translated)：

    Since the announcement of 27 cases of 2019-nCov by Wuhan Municipal Health Commission on December 31, 2019, the epidemic has spread all over the world. On January 30, 2020, the World Health Organization also declared the coronavirus outbreak an 'public health emergency of international concern Global Public Health Emergency' for the sixth time in the history. Apart from China, the epidemic is most severe in the United States, Brazil, India, Britain, France, Italy, Iran, Russia, and Peru. Most patients have symptoms of fever, dry cough, and burnout. About one-third of patients have shortness of breath, and other less symptoms include loss of taste or smell, muscle pain, sore throat, diarrhea, and etc.
